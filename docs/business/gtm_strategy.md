# Seed2Success: Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. The "Cold Start" Problem
We have a chicken-and-egg problem: The AI needs farmer data to become accurate, but farmers won't use the app if the AI isn't accurate.

## 2. Phase 1: The Pilot (Months 1-6)
- **Target:** 5,000 Farmers in a concentrated, single-crop region (e.g., Soybean farmers in Central India).
- **Strategy:** Do NOT rely on Facebook ads. We will partner with a local NGO or Farmer Producer Organization (FPO). We provide the NGO with the software for free; their field agents install it on the farmers' phones during their weekly visits.

## 3. Phase 2: The Data Flywheel (Months 6-12)
- **Target:** 50,000 Farmers.
- **Strategy:** Introduce localized Voice AI in the regional dialect. Run WhatsApp marketing campaigns showcasing success stories (e.g., "Farmer Ravi avoided Blight using Seed2Success"). Implement a referral program (invite 3 farmers, get priority SMS weather alerts).

## 4. Phase 3: B2B Enterprise Sales (Months 12-18)
- **Target:** 3 major Agri-businesses (Fertilizer, Seed, or Tractor companies).
- **Strategy:** Armed with 50,000 active farmers, we approach Enterprise clients. We pitch them our API. Instead of building their own ML division from scratch, they pay us $10k/month to integrate our yield prediction endpoints directly into their existing corporate software.
